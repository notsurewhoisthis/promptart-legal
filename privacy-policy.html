Introduction

PromptArt ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Photos: Images you select from your photo library or capture using the camera for transformation
  • Custom Prompts: Text descriptions you provide for custom transformations

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device identifier, iOS version, app version
  • Usage Data: Features used, transformation styles selected, frequency of use
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status, purchase history (handled by Apple and RevenueCat)
  • Authentication Data: Anonymous Firebase user ID

Information from Third Parties

  • Apple Sign In: If you choose to use Sign in with Apple, we receive your chosen display name and a unique identifier
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and purchase validation data

How We Use Your Information

Data Processing and Storage

Photo Processing

  • Photos are temporarily uploaded to our secure servers for AI processing
  • Images are processed using Google's Gemini API
  • Original and transformed images are deleted from our servers immediately after processing
  • We do not store your photos permanently on our servers

Data Storage Locations

  • Firebase (Google Cloud): User data, usage statistics - United States
  • RevenueCat: Subscription data - United States
  • Local Device: Transformed images, app preferences

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information in the following situations:

Service Providers

  • Google (Firebase & Gemini): Authentication, database, AI processing
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Apple: App distribution, payments

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transfers
  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

Data Retention

Your Privacy Rights

You can update your information through the app or by contacting us.

Deletion

European users have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to object to processing and the right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities.

PromptArt is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

You can opt-out of analytics collection through your device's privacy settings.
